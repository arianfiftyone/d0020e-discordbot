Create a poll with up to five options, with one right answer to the question. 
At least three arguments must be given. These are then randomly outputted due to the constraint input of arguments.

arguments:
correct,
alt1,
alt2,


usage in chat:
/multipoll [question], [correct], [alt1], ..., [alt3]

example:
/multipoll what is the capital of Sweden?, Stockholm, Gothenburg, Luleå, Malmö, Umeå
